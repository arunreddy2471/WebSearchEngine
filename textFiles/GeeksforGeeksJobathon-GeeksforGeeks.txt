Job-A-Thon is a coding contest organized by GeeksforGeeks for organizations that are looking to hire top coders. For this, organizations can directly contact us through our official email address, or they can submit their queries through the below-mentioned form, a representative from the GeeksforGeeks’ team will reach out to the organizations. You can share the information using this form : Hire with Job-A-Thon: A company participating in Jobathon will get the following benefits and services:- Posting of openings on the Jobs portal page. After the relevant info will be shared by the participating organizations, their openings will be listed on the portal. Once the openings are listed on the page, the registration window will open for the candidates. https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/events/rec/job-a-thon Promotions: To spread the information about the challenge to the audience, a promotional campaign will take place. Organizations will be able to reach out to the wider audience that is in millions, and new segments. The promotional part will include Bulk emails to the targeted user segments, etc. An article on GeeksforGeeks main website which talks about the benefits of joining the organization like culture, vision, etc. (Please note that the content of this article is to be provided by the organization. Our team will make the appropriate edits and publish.) In case of Fresher Jobs or internships, collaboration with colleges. college placement coordinators, TPOs, and student bodies. Conducting of contest: The contest will be conducted on the 21st of every month. A team of GeeksforGeeks will be on standby to check if the contest is going on smoothly and there are no hiccups. The contest will consist of 4 parts: Our team of experienced and talented SDEs has worked on these problems and the structure. 3 DSA coding problems: Easy, Medium Hard – 100 marks 5 MCQ on Programming Logic – 25 marks 5 MCQ on Logical Reasoning – 25 marks 5 MCQ on Quantitative Aptitude – 25 marks Total 18 questions, Total 175 marks. Shortlisting screening of the relevant profiles: Once the contest is conducted, the GeeksforGeeks team will work on to screen the data of the candidates who appeared in the contest. Then further processes (Plagiarism check etc) will be implemented to sort out the relevant profiles. An initial round of shortlisting will be conducted by GeeksforGeeks’ jobs portal team, then the data will be shared with the participating organizations. This whole after contest process may take up to 1-2 business days. Costing: The total cost of all the services included will be: With promotional services: INR 35,000 + GST Without promotional services: INR 10,000 + GST Exclusive Plan: A Tailor made contest designed specifically for your organizations need with end to end assistance from team GeekesforGeeks during whole process. This is specifically focused on sourcing the best talent for your organization. Costing: With Promotions – INR 50,000 + GST Without promotions – INR 15,000 + GST But for the organizations taking part in Jobathon for the first time, the costing will be Zero!
