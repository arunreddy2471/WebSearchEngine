Are you a DSA Philomath? If yes, Do you want to spend your weekend productively this summer vacation? So, GeeksforGeeks has come up with a solution DSA Weekly Contest – GFG Summer Break Challenge. Solve questions and bag prizes. Feels Great, Right? The perks of participating in this challenge are you become placement-ready as most of the questions asked in this contest are interview-level based. Also, these DSA Based coding contests will help you to clear the coding round in hiring challenges. Let’s have a quick go-through of the challenge. Contest Details GeeksforGeeks is organizing a set of 9 contests, based on different DSA topics, that will take place every Saturday during your summer break i.e., June and July 2022. So, buckle up your seats as this is the perfect opportunity to excel this placement season. Every Saturday 7 to 9 PM (June 2022 to July 2022) Total Sets: 9 Time Duration: 7 to 9 PM IST Every Set Contains: 3 – 4 DSA Questions Results Announcement Date: 1st week of August Registration Link: GFG Summer Break Challenge Note: Results will be declared according to the LeaderBoard score which is based on the maximum number of questions you solve in all the 9 contests. Also, there should be no plagiarism as each submission will undergo a plagiarism check. Rewards Position Prizes 1st position Smartwatch + Pens + Stickers + Mentorship session on Social Media 2nd position Lenovo tablet + Pens + Stickers 3rd position Earbuds + Pens + Stickers Topic Plan For Contests Date Topic 4 June Array, Matrix 11 June Searching, Sorting 18 June Strings 25 June Link List 2 July Stack, Queue 9 July Tree, BST 16 July Graph 23 July Greedy, Heap 30 July Dynamic Programming Instructions Each contest will help you prepare for DSA-based coding rounds in interviews. You will be asked to solve 3-4 problems based on data structures and algorithms in 2 hours. You will have access to hints. Just like in a real interview, asking for hints may have a negative impact on your overall score. You can solve the questions in C++, Java, and Python. Each submission will be tested on our critical test data. Get ready to bag exciting prizes by solving questions on DSA through the DSA Weekly Contest – GFG Summer Break Challenge.
